Feedback: C++ Audio Plugin

In college, I wanted to learn how to make VST3 plugins for music production. For this project, I used the JUCE C++ framework to make a plugin for guitar playing. The plugin tracks the signal's pitch and synthesizes a tone to mimic the feedback of a traditional electric guitar through an live amp.

Bootstrap: Wearable FX

Guitar effects are usually controlled by pedals. These analog devices are typically designed in a binary fashion (either on or off). I wanted to make an instrument controller that felt more expressive and natural. I 3D printed straps and used a microcontroller to make my final device.
